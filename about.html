About

The Science Journal is a student-run news organization that covers the scientific research, achievements, and initiatives of Brophy student and faculty members. In addition, the Science Journal is committed to improving scientific literacy and advocacy on Brophy’s campus.

If you have any questions or proposals, please contact the Science Journal of Brophy at sciencejournal@brophybroncos.org .
